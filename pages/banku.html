How To Prepare Ghanaian Banku With Grilled Tilapia

PREP TIME

15 MIN

COOK TIME

1 HR 15 MIN

SERVINGS

3 PEOPLE

Ingredients

Ingredients for grilled tilapia

  • 3 medium sized tialpia (fish)
  • 2 medium sized onions
  • 2 medium sized tomatoes
  • Some fresh pepper(green)
  • 1 small ginger
  • 2 small garlic
  • 1 lemon
  • 3 tbs of salt

Ingredients for banku

  • 2 cups of corn dough
  • 1 cup of cassava dough
  • 1 tbs of salt

Instructions

    Instructions to make grilled tilapia

  • Take the scales and fins off the tilapia
  • Please wash your tilapia with water. Clean it with the lemon and wash it again
  • Blend the pepper, garlic, ginger and onion.
  • Pour into a bowl, add the salt
  • Apply the mixture on the fish(under the grills and over). Then spray the oil onto the tilapia. Place it on the grill.
  • Turn it timely and spray the oil for easy grilling
  • Lastly add the remaining grind pepper mixture with the tomatoes and blend
  • Pour it onto a plate and garnish it with slice onion (green pepper and tomato are optional). This is called meko by te Ashantis.

    • Instructions to make the banku

  • Mix all ingredients with 4 cups of water in a pot.
  • Place the pot on fire and stir till it becomes heavy. Cover the pot and let it simmer for some time. Stir again.
  • It's ready when the colour changes and it gives off nice smell. Take it out and mould into small balls.
  • Keep in warmer container for freshness because it is served hot ♨️ with ground pepper or stew or soup
  • Now serve the banku with the meko with your nicely grilled tilapa.
